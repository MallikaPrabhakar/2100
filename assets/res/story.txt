It is the year 2100 and Corona Virus is growing stronger day by day.
Society has reached an apocalyptic situation and the world is now divided into a clan of 10 people each.
It is the survival of the fittest and government has been overthrown.
A new cure has been developed but the number of vaccines developed is very less.
Two clans enter a contest to gain vaccines where the whichever representative of the clan gets 10 vaccines/flags first gets vaccines for their clan whereas the other clan perishes.
Avoid covid-19 infested areas, maintain good health, avoid your rival's deadly corona shots and collect the flags fastest.
Hope you survive!
